Sencilia App

Sencilia's 'Flow sensors' are designed to alleviate nurses' workload by providing precise flow rate information. The objective was to develop user-friendly healthcare software for nurses, incorporating expert reviews, interviews, and iterative design. The final prototype, refined through desk research and user testing, successfully meets both client and nurse requirements. It efficiently transmits sensor data, includes notification alarms for critical situations, and organizes application levels for enhanced usability.

Texel

I have been working on the design of the website for Hotel Den Burg, located in a prime location on Texel, one of the four largest islands in the Netherlands. My task was to create the entire design from scratch, using Figma for the development process. The website design reflects the hotel's commitment to quality and hospitality, providing users with an intuitive and visually appealing platform to explore and book their stay. Additionally, I incorporated user feedback and analytics ensuring an exceptional user experience.

Elio Hotel

Elio Hotel is a personal website design project that I developed from scratch. Located on an imaginary island, this project aimed to provide me with hands-on experience in the full development process. Beginning with creating the design in Figma, I later implemented it using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This project not only showcased my ability to design layouts but also demonstrated my proficiency in translating designs into a fully functional website. It also helped me enhance my problem-solving skills.
